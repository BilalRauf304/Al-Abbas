About Al Abbas Online Quran Academy

Al Abbas Online Quran Academy is an international Islamic and academic institute dedicated to providing high-quality online Quran education to students of all ages.

Our mission is to spread the knowledge of the Holy Quran across the world with proper Tajweed, understanding, and Islamic teachings under the supervision of qualified male and female teachers.

We also provide academic subjects like English, Math, and Science to help students succeed in both Islamic and worldly education.
